About SmartMail Expense Tracker

Simplifying expense management through email automation

Our Mission

SmartMail Expense Tracker was created to simplify personal and business expense tracking. We believe that managing expenses should be automatic, not manual. By leveraging email receipts that you already receive, we make expense tracking effortless and accurate.

Our goal is to help individuals and businesses gain better insights into their spending patterns without the hassle of manual data entry.

How It Works

1. Email Integration

Connect your email account securely using industry-standard IMAP protocols. We support Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, and other IMAP-compatible services.

2. Automatic Scanning

Our system scans your incoming emails for receipts and purchase confirmations. We identify key information like amounts, merchants, and dates using advanced pattern recognition.

3. Smart Categorization

Using machine learning algorithms, we automatically categorize expenses into appropriate categories like Food, Transportation, Shopping, Utilities, etc.

4. Data Insights

View your spending patterns through interactive charts and reports. Export your data, set up budget alerts, and gain valuable financial insights.

Security & Privacy

We take your privacy and security seriously. Here's how we protect your data:

  • Encrypted Connections: All email connections use SSL/TLS encryption to ensure your data is secure during transmission.
  • App Passwords: We recommend using app-specific passwords for email access, which are more secure than regular passwords.
  • Local Storage: Your expense data is stored locally in your browser and our secure database.
  • No Data Sharing: We never share your personal information or email data with third parties.
  • Transparent Code: Our application logic is transparent and follows security best practices.
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures to protect against new threats.

Getting Started

To get started with SmartMail Expense Tracker, follow these simple steps:

  1. Create a free account on our platform
  2. Go to Email Settings and add your email account
  3. For Gmail/Outlook, use app passwords for enhanced security
  4. Allow the system to sync your emails (happens automatically every few minutes)
  5. View your automatically categorized expenses on the Dashboard
  6. Add manual expenses for any receipts not received via email
  7. Generate reports to analyze your spending patterns

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my email password stored securely?

Yes, we store email app passwords encrypted in our database. We never store your actual email password. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your credentials.

Which email providers are supported?

We support Gmail, Outlook/Office 365, Yahoo Mail, and any email provider that offers IMAP access. Most modern email services support IMAP.

How often are emails checked for new receipts?

By default, we check for new emails every 5 minutes. You can adjust this frequency in the settings or manually sync anytime with the click of a button.

Can I manually add expenses?

Yes! You can manually add expenses through the dashboard in addition to automatic email extraction. This is useful for cash transactions or receipts received through other channels.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, SmartMail Expense Tracker is a web application that works on all devices. The interface is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile browsers.

How do I export my expense data?

You can export your expense data in CSV, JSON, or Excel format from the Reports page. This makes it easy to import into other financial software or spreadsheets.

Is there a cost for using SmartMail?

SmartMail Expense Tracker offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features like advanced analytics and unlimited email accounts are available in our paid plans.
